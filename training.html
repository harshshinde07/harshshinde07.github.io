Frequently asked Questions

Why should I take your courses?

All courses taught by skilled professionals having more than 3 years of industry experience working in the industry.

We go beyond the college level, help you get the basics right and become skillful and confident in your ability.

We also take limited seats to ensure rich learning experience.

Yes! The benefits that you get are actually a lot more than the price you pay for!

When you enroll to any of our paid courses, you are entitled for the following benefits:

  • Hand holding support for all your doubts related to the course.
  • Access to active community wherein you can ask for help, collaborate on projects and become better engineer yourself.
  • A FREE 1:1 Session to solve all your doubts related to Career guidance, Resume review, Interview preparation, Project guidance, Mock interviews or anything tech.
  • A 10% discount on all of our courses!
  • Referral support for getting a job. (If available)

How are the courses taught

All courses will take place live online on weekends. The links will be shared prior to the class.

What will be the duration of a single online class?

A single online class duration can range anywhere between 1-2 hours.

What is the course structure?

All courses will start from the basics, will have practical examples with live coding as well as assignments for hands-on experience to ensure rich learning experience. We also have discussion sessions at the end of each class where all your doubts will be solved.

Any prerequisites for joining your courses?

Almost all of our courses start from the ground up, building strong basics and then moving to the advanced concepts.

All we ask is proper dedication during the class and the assignments to get the most out of our courses.

Please refrain from joining the courses if you are not interested in deeper understanding of the concepts and are not ready to put in the hard work.

I have enrolled for the course, what's next?

Sit back and relax, it is our responsibility to take it from here.

You will be added to a WhatsApp group with your fellow students and all the updates will be provided there.

Please note that we require at least 10-12 people to start the course, so feel free to invite your friends!
